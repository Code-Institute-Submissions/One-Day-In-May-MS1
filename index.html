ITFC completed the greatest trilogy in play-off history and returned to the Premier League.

Introduction

The passing of the 20th anniversary of our exciting promotion to the Premier League in May 2000 gives us a chance to reflect on the incredible trilogy of play-off games and the key men involved. So whether you’re an ITFC fan here to relive the moment or a younger fan wanting to learn more, you will experience the extraordinary journey of Ipswich Town’s play-off success in May 2000!
